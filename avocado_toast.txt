Avocado Toast
This is the easiest avocado toast recipe you’ll ever need to know.Memorize the recipe, then use it as a jumping off point for your favorite avocado toast riffs.Put an egg on it, top it with tuna salad,or crown it with tomatoes for a healthy and satisfying meal.
Avocado,lemon juice,salt,black pepper,slice of whole grain bread,olive oil
In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.Gently mash with the back of a fork.Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture.Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle over desired toppings.
