Black Bean Tostadas
Toasted tortillas topped with flavorful black beans and fresh vegetables make a quick and delicious light meal.This is also a great recipe for entertaining, because of its ease and high yield.To spice things up, top black bean tostadas with slices of fresh jalapeño just before serving.
10 6-inch tortillas,1 15-oz. cans black beans,2 tablespoons lemon juice,1 1/2 tablespoons onion powder,1 1/2 tablespoons garlic powder,1 teaspoon ground cumin,sea salt,ground black pepper,2 cups shredded romaine lettuce,1 green onion,1 medium tomato,3 tablespoons fresh cilantro.
Preheat oven to 350°F.Arrange tortillas in a single layer on baking sheets and bake for 20 minutes or until crisp.Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan combine the black beans, lemon juice, onion powder, garlic powder, and ground cumin.Add 1/2 cup of water and cook over medium heat 10 minutes, adding additional water, 1 to 2 tablespoon at a time, if needed to prevent sticking.Mash bean mixture to reach desired consistency, adding additional water if desired to make creamier and season with salt and pepper.To assemble, spread tortillas with bean mixture, using 1/4 cup for each tortilla.Lastly, top with lettuce, green onions, tomato, and cilantro.
